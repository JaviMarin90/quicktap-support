03 — Legal Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Quick Tap: Auto Clicker Tool ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Quick Tap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. The app runs entirely on your device with no server-side processing.

Data Storage

All app settings and configurations (such as tap speed, positions, and intervals) are stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Quick Tap does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

Quick Tap does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data at all, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
